Valorant: What is it?

VALORANT is a free-to-play 5v5 character-based tactical shooter where precise gunplay meets unique agent abilities. Developed by Riot Games, the game challenges players to outsmart and out-maneuver their opponents across various maps and modes. Whether you are playing on PC, Xbox Series X|S, or PlayStation 5, every shot counts and every ability can turn the tide of battle. Defy the limits and join the fight.

THE HIERARCHY OF POWER

From Iron to Radiant, prove your skill across nine prestige tiers and earn your place among legends.

O "Elo" is your competitive ranking in VALORANT. It represents your level of technical and tactical skill, measured through victories and individual performance. The more you win, the higher you climb the hierarchy, facing increasingly challenging opponents.
